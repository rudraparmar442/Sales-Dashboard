# Sales-Performance-Dashboard

**Tools:** Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) • Power BI • Excel  
**Domain:** Sales Analytics | Business Intelligence  
**Dataset:** 5,000 rows of retail sales data (2024)

---

## Project Overview
Analyzed one full year of sales data across 5 regions,
8 products, and 8 sales representatives to identify 
revenue trends, profit margins, and top performers —
presented via an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Key Insights

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Revenue | ₹399M |
| Total Profit | ₹136M |
| Avg Profit Margin | 33.95% |
| Total Orders | 5,000 |
| Best Region | North |
| Top Product | Laptop |

---

##  Business Recommendations
1. North region leads — replicate strategy in other regions
2. Accessories have best margin — bundle with Electronics
3. Laptop drives most revenue — prioritize stock & promotions
4. December peak — plan Q4 inventory in advance
5. Low discount orders show better margins — review discount policy
